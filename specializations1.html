Specializations

Specializations on Coursera are multi-course programs that allow learners to deeply engage with a specific subject area. These are particularly helpful for individuals looking to become experts in a particular field or gain a comprehensive understanding of a topic. Focused Curriculum: A specialization is a sequence of related courses designed to build expertise step-by-step. For example, a specialization in Data Science might include courses on programming, data analysis, machine learning, and visualization.
