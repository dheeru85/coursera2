Registration

Help Center: Visit the Coursera Help Center. Here you can find answers to common questions or submit a request to their support team. Submit a Request: From the Help Center, scroll to the bottom of the page, and you'll find an option to "Submit a request." You can choose the type of issue you're experiencing (e.g., account issues, course-related questions) and get assistance. Social Media: Coursera is active on social media platforms like Twitter (@coursera), Facebook, and LinkedIn. You can try messaging them or commenting on their posts for assistance. Coursera Community: You can also join the Coursera Community to ask questions, connect with other learners, or get advice.
