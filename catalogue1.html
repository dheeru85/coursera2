Catalogue

Catalogue Coursera offers a wide range of online learning options, including: Courses: Available in fields like Business, Computer Science, Health, Social Sciences, Arts, Data Science, and Languages. Specializations: Series of related courses to master a skill. Professional Certificates: Job-ready programs in fields like IT, Data Science, and Marketing. MasterTrack Certificates: Segments of master’s degrees offering university credits. Degree Programs: Full online bachelor’s and master’s degrees in partnership with universities. Free and Paid Options: Free auditing available, paid certificates provide additional features. Coursera for Business: Corporate training and upskilling programs. Coursera Plus: A subscription that gives access to most courses and certificates.
